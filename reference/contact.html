Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Q
Where can I buy Jeevan Rekha oils?
Jeevan Rekha oils are available at select retail stores across West Bengal and can be ordered directly through our website or by calling our office. We're continuously expanding our retail presence — contact us for the nearest stockist.
Q
Do you offer bulk or trade pricing?
Yes. We supply to restaurants, food manufacturers, caterers, and institutional buyers at trade pricing. We offer volumes from 15L to large bulk supplies. Select "Bulk Order" or "Distributor / Trade" in the contact form and our sales team will reach out within one day.
Q
Can I become a distributor for Jeevan Rekha?
We welcome distribution partnerships across India. We are actively building our distribution network in states outside West Bengal. Send us an enquiry with your region, current trade network, and preferred product lines — our team will evaluate and respond promptly.
Q
Are your oils FSSAI certified?
Yes. All Jeevan Rekha oils are manufactured under a valid FSSAI licence and every production batch is independently lab-tested for purity, nutritional parameters, and food safety compliance before being packaged and dispatched.
Q
What pack sizes are available?
Our consumer packs are available in 500ml, 1L, 2L, and 5L. For trade and institutional buyers, we offer 15L tins and bulk packaging. Custom packaging and private label options are available for large volume orders — please enquire directly.
Q
Can I visit the manufacturing plant?
Yes, authorised trade visits can be arranged by appointment. Our Burdwan facility is open for visits Monday to Friday. Please call +91 74392 89709 or email info@abudyog.in at least 3 working days in advance to schedule your visit.
