Our Story
Born in the Lanes of North Kolkata

Shuddha began as a small family venture in Shyambazar, born from a simple belief — that Bengali cooking deserved an oil as wholesome as the cuisine it carried. Our founder, Smt. Kamala Ghosh, started pressing rice bran oil in her backyard kitchen in 1994.

Three decades later, Shuddha has grown into one of West Bengal's most trusted edible oil brands — but our roots remain unchanged. Every bottle still carries the same promise: pure, natural, and made with love from the land that feeds India.
