Our Founding Story

Built on Decades of
Earned Knowledge

Before Jeevan Rekha existed as a brand, our founding team spent over four decades as major suppliers of rice bran and its derivatives to solvent extraction units across India. We understood the raw material, the processing, the supply chain — and its flaws — better than most.

That experience revealed a gap: families deserved access to the same quality of oil that industrial buyers demanded. So in February 2019, AB Udyog Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated with a single, clear mission — to bring genuinely pure, nutritionally superior edible oils directly to Indian consumers under the Jeevan Rekha brand.

"We don't just sell oil. We sell the confidence that what goes into your pan is exactly what we say it is — nothing added, nothing hidden."

Today, we remain one of the most reliable suppliers of rice bran oil to the snack and namkeen industry across India — while simultaneously serving thousands of households who have made Jeevan Rekha their kitchen's trusted partner.
