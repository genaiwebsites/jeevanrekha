# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Health-conscious Indian families, quality-seeking home cooks, and prospective B2B distributors who want unadulterated, premium cooking oils. They are looking for nutritional transparency (like Gamma-Oryzanol levels, Omega-3 content, and smoke points) and traditional culinary authenticity.

## Product Purpose
Establish Jeevan Rekha as the gold standard of premium, unadulterated edible oils by merging 40+ years of industrial rice bran refining heritage with modern science. The website acts as a premium digital showcase that builds consumer trust and details our scientific manufacturing processes.

## Brand Personality
"Purity Engineering" — Scientific, Premium, Authentic. The voice is informative, confident, and scientifically transparent, avoiding cheap FMCG slogans. It reflects the convergence of time-tested cold-pressing traditions and state-of-the-art refining engineering.

## Anti-references
- **Generic FMCG templates**: Flat layouts, cluttered grid sections, cheap stock clip-art, and low-contrast body text.
- **Warm-cream AI defaults**: Saturated cream, sand, linen, or bone-white backgrounds that make the website feel like a generic 2026 AI-generated template.
- **Typical industrial sites**: Dry, sterile, or overly corporate layouts that fail to evoke kitchen warmth or premium quality.
- **AI Purple Slop**: Lazy, high-vibrancy purple meshes and floating neon bubbles that serve no purpose other than decorative filler.

## Design Principles
- **Scientific Transparency**: Lead with precise metrics (e.g., 254°C Smoke Point, molecular formulas like *γ-Oryzanol*) in technical monospace typefaces. Facts, not claims, build true authority.
- **Restrained Brand Presence**: Leverage the royal purple (`#4B2685`) and bright yellow (`#FEDC06`) palette with high precision and restraint. Colors define sections and actions; they should never flood the screen like a default AI generator output.
- **Tactile Physics**: Give UI components weight and responsiveness through smooth bezier translations, scale shifts, and interactive custom cursor states that make the product bottles feel physically present.
- **Editorial Rhythm**: Alternate between bold serif display typefaces (`Fraunces`) for storytelling and highly readable sans-serif typefaces (`Instrument Sans`) for technical or body content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Full compliance with WCAG 2.2 AA standards, ensuring strong color contrast (body text ≥4.5:1, headings/icons ≥3:1).
- Accessible keyboard navigation and ARIA attributes for dynamic SPA view switches.
- Explicit `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` rules for all custom GSAP, custom cursor, and CSS transitions.
